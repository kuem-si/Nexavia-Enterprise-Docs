## Zoom
On devices with mouse, rotating mouse wheel  will zoom in or out.<br />
On touch-based devices, using spread and pinch gesture result in zoom in or out.

## Pan
On devices with mouse, dragging mouse results in panning.<br />
On touch-based devices, dragging gesture on the screen results in panning.
