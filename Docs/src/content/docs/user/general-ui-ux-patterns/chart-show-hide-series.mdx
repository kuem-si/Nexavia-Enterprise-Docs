---
title: Chart - Show & Hide Series
---

Showing and hiding specific series on chart can be done by clicking on time series inside chart legend.

<img src={uiUxPatternChartLegend.src} alt="ui-ux-pattern-chart-legend-link" />

:::tip
When exporting or printing chart with hidden series, only shown series will be visible on export/print.
:::
