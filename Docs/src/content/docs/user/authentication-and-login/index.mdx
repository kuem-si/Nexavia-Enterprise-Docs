### First, click the Login link to start the login flow
This will take you to the login screen

<img src={startIndexPageLoginLink.src} alt="start-index-page-login-link" />

### When you are accessing the application for the first time, you have to enter your Tenant name

Press the "Switch" button to open the Tenant selection menu
<img src={loginPageSelectTenant.src} alt="login-page-select-tenant" />

### Enter your Tenant name into the text box and click the "Save" button

<img src={tenantSelectionMenu.src} alt="tenant-selection-menu" />

### To complete the login process, enter your credentials into the provided fields and click the "Login" button
You can either enter your email address or username.

<img src={loginPageCredentials.src} alt="login-page-credentials" />

### If the credentials are valid, you will be redirected to the front page of the application.

<img src={homePage.src} alt="home-page" />
